Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Box Canyon AI LLC ("Box Canyon AI," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and their customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our AI-powered voice receptionist service.

1. Information We Collect

When our AI receptionist (Elliot) handles a call on behalf of a client, we collect the following information from callers:

  • Full name
  • Service address (street, city, state)
  • Callback phone number
  • Description of the service issue
  • Urgency level of the service request

We also collect business contact information from our clients during the onboarding process, including business name, phone number, and email address.

2. How We Use the Information

Information collected during intake calls is used solely to:

  • Notify the client (trade business owner) of incoming service requests via SMS
  • Log service request details for the client's records
  • Improve the performance and accuracy of our AI receptionist

We do not use caller information for marketing purposes, and we do not sell or share caller data with third parties for any commercial purpose.

3. SMS Notifications

Box Canyon AI sends automated SMS messages to registered clients notifying them of new service requests captured by Elliot. These messages contain the lead details collected during the intake call. Clients who sign up for our service consent to receive these notifications as part of the service.

Message and data rates may apply. To opt out of SMS notifications, reply STOP to any message. To request support, reply HELP or contact us at hello@boxcanyonai.com.

4. Data Retention

Call intake data is retained for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill our obligations to clients. Clients may request deletion of their data by contacting us directly.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our services use industry-standard encryption and access controls.

6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to deliver our services, including voice AI infrastructure, SMS delivery, and data storage. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.

7. Your Rights

Callers or clients who wish to access, correct, or delete information we hold about them may contact us at:

Email: hello@boxcanyonai.com
Website: boxcanyonai.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@boxcanyonai.com.
